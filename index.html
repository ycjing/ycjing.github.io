Biography

I am currently a research intern at the Department of Computer Vision Technology (VIS), Baidu Inc.

Prior to that, I received my MS in Computer Science at the Microsoft Visual Perception Laboratory (VIPA) of Zhejiang University (ZJU) and Alibaba-Zhejiang University Joint Research Institute of Frontier Technologies (AZFT), fortunately advised by Prof. Mingli Song and Prof. Yizhou Yu.

I will become a PhD student at the University of Sydney, advised by Prof. Dacheng Tao and Prof. Xinchao Wang.
