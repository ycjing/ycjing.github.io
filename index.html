Biography

I am currently a first-year PhD student at the University of Sydney, fortunately advised by Prof. Dacheng Tao and Prof. Xinchao Wang.

Prior to the University of Sydney, I received my MS in Computer Science at the Microsoft Visual Perception Laboratory (VIPA) of Zhejiang University (ZJU) and Alibaba-Zhejiang University Joint Research Institute of Frontier Technologies (AZFT), advised by Prof. Mingli Song and Prof. Yizhou Yu.
