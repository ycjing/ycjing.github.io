Biography

I am a Postdoc in the School of Computer Science at the University of Sydney, advised by Prof. Dacheng Tao and Prof. Seokhee Hong. I am doing research on Graph Intelligence (i.e., Graph Neural Networks). I obtained my Ph.D. degree from the same school in September 2023 under the supervision of Prof. Dacheng Tao.

Research Interests

Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Information Visualisation
