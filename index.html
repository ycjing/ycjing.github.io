Yongcheng Jing

Biography

I was a Postdoc in the School of Computer Science at the University of Sydney, advised by Prof. Dacheng Tao and Prof. Seokhee Hong. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from the same school in September 2023 under the supervision of Prof. Dacheng Tao.

Research Interests

My research currently focuses on Large Language Model Reasoning, with a particular emphasis on its Healthcare Applications:

  • Medical Large Language Model
  • Graph-augmented Reasoning
  • Reasoning Robustness and Safety
